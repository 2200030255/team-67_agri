sustainable farming

it means farming in sustainable ways meeting societys present food and textile needs,with out compromising the ability for current or future generations to meet their needs

shifting agriculture

crops are grown for two to three years and then the lank is abandoned as the fertility of the soil decreases and the process is repeated

plantation agriculture

crops grown in plantation agriculture have a life cycle of more than two types

intensive farming

Both of crop plants and of animals,with higher levels of input and output per unit of agriculture land area

dry agriculture

practice of growing crops with out irrigation in aeras which receive an annual rainfall of 750mm-500mm or even less

mixed and multiple cropping

It is the practice of growing two or more crops together

crop roatation

It is growing of number of crops one after the other in a fixed rotation to maintain fertility of the soil

terrace cultivation

the hill and mountain slopes are cut to form terrace and the land is used in the same way as in permanent agriculture
